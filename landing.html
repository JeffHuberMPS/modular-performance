From the Founder

I've trained kickboxing for 10 years, competed, won, and built my body through years of lifting, relentless training, and obsessive daily discipline.

Outside the gym, I built my career in high-performance sales through persuasion, communication, and disciplined execution.

I believe discipline extends beyond the gym, which is why I track everything from training and recovery to performance and finances with precision and intent.

But every part of my life felt fragmented.

My workouts were in one app while my habits were on a whiteboard, and my finances lived in spreadsheets I rebuilt every month, all trapped in different systems that never truly connected.

So I designed the MPS system to bring structure to every area of disciplined living.

MPS wasn't built for casual users or people chasing motivation. It was built for people who take discipline seriously and demand more from themselves every day.

It was built for the man who wants structure, discipline, and precision in every area of life, from training and recovery to mindset, money, and faith.

Unified systems. One vision. Total clarity.
