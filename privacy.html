Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Modular Performance Systems ("MPS", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our app and website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in (via Google), we receive your name, email address, and profile photo.
  • Data you log. The content you enter into the app — workouts, habits, recovery/sleep, expenses, and journal entries — is stored so the app can show it back to you.
  • Payment information. If you subscribe, payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number; Stripe handles it directly.
  • Technical data. Basic information your browser sends (such as device type and general usage) that helps the app run and improve.

How we use your information

  • To provide the app and show you your own tracking data.
  • To sync your data across your devices.
  • To process subscriptions and manage your plan.
  • To maintain security and improve the product.

How your data is stored

Your account and tracking data are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore). Security rules restrict access so that you can only read and write your own data — no other user can see it.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers that make the app work — Google / Firebase (sign-in and storage), Stripe (payments), and Vercel (hosting) — and only as needed to provide the service.

Your choices

  • You can view and edit your tracking data at any time inside the app.
  • You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account by emailing us.

Children

MPS is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
